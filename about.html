             ABOUT US

Who are we?

SRMD start-ups is a website platform by Kumasi Hive and Shenzhen Open Innovation Lab. From this platform you will learn about the new start-ups developed in the Shenzhen Ready Made Design program of Kumasi Hive and their work and latest developments will be shown in content.

This is an interactive platform that connects the new companies with electronic companies in Shenzhen - existing or potential partners - interested in open source collaboration. News, articles, questions and advice through the dedicated interactive forum can be shared by all partners, in order to create an open-source knowledge and practical work on AI-powered electronics, to further boost innovations between important collaborations.
